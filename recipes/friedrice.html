Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Fry eggs,garlics and onios
  2. Boil water, wait for 15 minutes
  3. When water boiled, put rice inside a pot
  4. After rice is ready, put rice in fried eggs and onions
  5. Let simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Serve
